The Challenge's Beginnings


The Fire Noodle Challenge

The Fire Noodle Challenge was started in February 2014 by a Youtube channel called “영국남자 Korean Englishman”. After receiving mail from fans, Josh (the YouTuber) noticed that many of the mailed packages contained “Fire Chicken Noodles”. After receiving the noodes, Josh decided to initiate a challenge to finish one serving of these noodles. Josh and his friends did this challenege, and then a couple days later, the video went viral quick. Thus the beginning of the Fire Noodle Challenge! If you’re looking to try the Fire Noodle Challenge yourself, you will need to get your hands on the noodles first. Due to the popularity it has become easier and easier to find the packages at local Asian markets, like J-town at Berkeley for example. I hope you all try this food. Don't be intimidated, who knows, you might actually really like this a lot!
